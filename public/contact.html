Contact Football Wordle

Football Wordle is an independent fan project. We read every message we receive and respond to most within a few business days.

General contact

For any question, comment, or feedback about the game:

webgames594@gmail.com

We typically respond within 2–4 business days. Please include a short description of your question in the subject line.

What to contact us about

  • Word list feedback. If you think a player or club name is missing, misspelled, or should be removed, email us with the name, the league, and a brief reason. We review all word list feedback before each weekly update.
  • Bug reports. If the game behaves unexpectedly — incorrect color hints, keyboard issues, layout problems on a specific device — describe what you saw and include your browser and device if possible.
  • Accessibility issues. We are committed to making Football Wordle accessible to all players. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know so we can fix it.
  • Suggestions. New game modes, additional leagues, feature ideas — we welcome all suggestions even if we can't implement everything.
  • Advertising enquiries. For advertising-related questions, include "Advertising" in the subject line.
  • Press or partnership enquiries. Include "Press" or "Partnership" in the subject line.

Before you write

These pages may already answer your question:

  • How to play — full rules, color hint guide, and FAQ for new players.
  • About the project — how the word lists are built and updated.
  • Privacy policy — how your data is (not) handled.
  • Blog — strategy guides and league overviews.

What we don't offer

Football Wordle is a small independent project. We are not able to:

  • Guarantee a specific name will be added to the word list.
  • Restore game stats that were lost when a player cleared their browser data.
  • Provide an API or data export of our word lists.
  • Offer real-time support or a live chat service.

We appreciate your understanding and are grateful to every player who takes the time to reach out.
