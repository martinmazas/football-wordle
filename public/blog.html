About this blog

Every article here is written by the Football Wordle team. We cover three things:

  • Strategy: how to approach each round more intelligently, from first guess to final attempt.
  • League knowledge: context about the leagues and competitions whose players and clubs appear in the game.
  • Editorial notes: when word lists change significantly, we explain what was added, removed, and why.
